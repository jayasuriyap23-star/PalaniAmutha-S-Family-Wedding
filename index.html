The Sacred Sequence

Order Of Events

Every ritual carries centuries of meaning — join us as we honour each one.

Ganapathi Pooja

Invoking Lord Ganesha to bless the beginning of all rituals.

Reception

Welcoming family and friends to celebrate together.

Nalangu

Playful pre-wedding rituals bringing the couple's families closer.

Mangala Snanam

The sacred ceremonial bath symbolising purity before the wedding.

Kashi Yatra

The groom's symbolic journey, gently persuaded to embrace married life.

Maalai Maatral

Exchange of garlands as the couple accepts one another.

Oonjal

The couple is seated on a swing and showered with blessings.

Kanyadhanam

The sacred giving away of the bride by her parents.

Mangalya Dharanam

The groom ties the sacred mangalsutra — the holiest moment of the wedding.

Sapthapathi

Seven sacred steps taken together around the holy fire.

Ashirvadam

Elders bless the newlyweds for a life of happiness.

Wedding Feast

A grand traditional feast served on banana leaves for all our guests.
