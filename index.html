✦ Our Story ✦

A New Chapter Begins

From two families and two journeys, a single path now begins. We are endlessly grateful to everyone who has shaped our story with love, laughter, and support along the way. As we take this sacred step together, we invite you — our cherished family and friends — to stand beside us, share in our joy, and bless us as we begin this beautiful new chapter of life.

✦ Celebration Timeline ✦

Every Sacred Moment

🌿 Day One — Pre-Wedding Celebrations
🪔

Ganapathi Pooja

A sacred beginning invoking Lord Ganesha's blessings to remove all obstacles from the path ahead.

🌼

Nalangu

A joyful, playful ritual filled with music, teasing games, and laughter between the couple and their families.

🎉

Reception & Celebrations

An evening of festivity, feasting, and heartfelt wishes as loved ones gather to celebrate the couple.

💍 Day Two — Wedding Ceremony
🛁

Mangala Snanam

The sacred ceremonial bath symbolizing purification before the rituals begin.

🚶

Kashi Yatra

A symbolic journey where the groom pretends to renounce worldly life, gently persuaded to return by the bride's family.

🌸

Maalai Maatral

The joyful exchange of garlands between the bride and groom, marking their acceptance of one another.

🤝

Kanyadhanam

The bride's father ceremonially gives her hand in marriage, entrusting her to her partner with blessings.

💛

Mangalya Dharanam

The groom ties the sacred thali around the bride's neck — the defining moment of the wedding.

👣

Sapthapathi

Seven sacred steps taken together around the holy fire, each one a vow for their shared life ahead.

🙏

Ashirvadam

Elders bless the newlywed couple, showering them with blessings for a prosperous life together.

🌺

Nagavalli Subhamuhurtham

An auspicious ritual marking the formal completion of the sacred wedding rites.

🍽️

Traditional Wedding Feast

A grand banquet served on banana leaves, celebrating the union with family and friends.
